Biography

I am a PhD Candidate at UC Berkeley’s Energy & Resources Group and a Researcher at the Energy Institute at Haas. My research interests are centered on applied empirical work at the intersection of energy and environmental economics, public policy and data science. My job market paper examines the economic costs of NIMBYism in the context of renewable energy projects.
