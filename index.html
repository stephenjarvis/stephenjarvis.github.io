Stephen Jarvis

Bio

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Mannheim Department of Economics. My PhD work was at UC Berkeley’s Energy & Resources Group where I was also a Researcher at the Energy Institute at Haas.

My research interests are centered on energy and environmental economics, public policy and data science. My job market paper examines the economic costs of NIMBYism in the context of renewable energy projects.
