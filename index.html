Biography

I am a PhD student at UC Berkeley’s Energy & Resources Group and a Researcher at the Energy Institute at Haas. My research interests are centered on energy and environmental economics, with a particular focus on electricity markets. I am also exploring novel applications of econometric and machine learning methods in this space, including how these can be blended with more traditional optimization model approaches.
